Why we donate

At Pawthways we reserve 40% of revenue for verified animal rescues. We believe in direct, traceable support that reaches animals and recovery efforts without unnecessary middlemen.

  • Direct impact — funds go to vetted rescues and emergency care.
  • Verifiable — receipts and CSV exports are published monthly.
  • Accountable — we keep a 4-step verification process and public proofs.

Short statement: We donate because rescued animals deserve fast, transparent aid — and your trust demands proof.

What we publish

  • Monthly aggregates — summary totals and timelines
  • Receipts / Proof PDFs — individual PDFs for each donation or transfer
  • CSV downloads — machine-readable exports for auditing
  • Beneficiaries list — vetted partners and status
  • Audit reports (when available) — independent reviews and notes

Privacy note: Public proofs have personally identifiable information (PII) removed or redacted. Legal review is required before any PII is published.
